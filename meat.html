Vietnamese Shaking Beef / Bo Luc Lac

"Bo" means beef, and "luc lac" means shaking, together we have shaking beef. This classic Vietnamese dish features tender cubes of beef that are seared over high heat and tossed in a savory sauce. The beef is juicy, and coated with a delightful mix of flavors from garlic, soy sauce, and a hint of sweetness. It's typically served over a bed of watercress or lettuce, sliced tomatoes and/or cucumber, and with a side of steamed rice. Simple, flavorful, and quick to prepare. It's a perfect meal for any day of the week.

Serves Prep Cook Total Time
4 servings 20 minutes 10 minutes 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin or tenderloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 medium yellow onion, cut into 2 pieces
  • 1 head of lettuce or watercress, for serving
  • Sliced tomatoes and cucumbers, for garnish
  • Steamed jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, add the beef cubes, oyster sauce, brown sugar, soy sauce, fish sauce, grated garlic, baking soda, ground black pepper, and vegetable oil. Mix well. Cover with an upturned plate, and let it marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes or overnight for best results.
  2. When you're ready to cook the beef, prepare your lettuce, sliced cucumber and sliced tomatoes.
  3. Heat a wok or skillet over medium-high heat until it's hot. Add the beef cubes to the skillet in a single layer. Let them sear without moving for about 2 minutes to develop a nice crust.
  4. Toss the beef cubes and continue to cook for another 2-3 minutes until they are browned on all sides but still medium-rare inside. Adjust cooking time based on your preferred doneness.
  5. Pour the sauce mixture over the beef and add water. Stir well to coat the beef evenly. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Remove from heat and let the beef rest for a few minutes.
  7. Serve the shaking beef over a bed of lettuce or watercress, garnished with sliced tomatoes and cucumbers. Accompany with steamed jasmine rice.
